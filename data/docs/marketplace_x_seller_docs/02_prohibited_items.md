# Marketplace X – Prohibited & Restricted Products

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy defines which products cannot be listed on Marketplace X and which products are subject to additional restrictions, approvals, or conditions. It applies to all sellers, regardless of account type, location, or sales volume.

Sellers are responsible for verifying the legality and compliance of each product in every destination country where they ship.

---

## 2. General Principles

- Products must be **legal**, **safe**, and **authentic**.
- Products must comply with all applicable **laws**, **regulations**, and **standards** in the origin and destination countries.
- Products must comply with all **Marketplace X policies**, including Listing Quality and Category Guidelines.
- If there is any uncertainty about legality or compliance, sellers must not list the product until clarification is obtained from legal counsel or relevant authorities.

Marketplace X may remove or block listings and take enforcement actions without prior notice if a product is suspected to violate this policy.

---

## 3. Prohibited Products

The following categories are strictly prohibited and may not be listed under any circumstances.

### 3.1 Illegal Items

- Items whose sale or distribution is illegal in any relevant jurisdiction.
- Stolen goods or items with serial numbers removed or altered.
- Items intended to facilitate illegal activities (e.g., illegal drug manufacturing equipment, tools for unauthorized access to computer systems).

### 3.2 Counterfeit and Infringing Products

- Counterfeit or unauthorized replicas of branded products.
- Items incorporating trademarks, logos, or copyrighted material without permission.
- Unauthorized copies of software, music, movies, books, or other digital content.
- Packaging or labels that misleadingly mimic another brand.

Sellers must be able to provide authentic purchase invoices or distribution agreements upon request.

### 3.3 Dangerous and Controlled Substances

- Illegal drugs and narcotics.
- Prescription medicines and controlled substances, unless explicitly authorized under a dedicated program.
- Unregulated or banned dietary supplements.
- High-risk chemicals restricted by law, including explosive precursors.
- Products marketed with claims to cure, treat, or prevent diseases without proper regulatory approval.

### 3.4 Weapons and Related Items

- Firearms and ammunition.
- Explosives, fireworks, and military-grade weaponry.
- Functional tasers, stun guns, and similar devices, where prohibited by law.
- Switchblades, disguised weapons, and other items classified as prohibited weapons in target countries.

Certain utility tools (e.g., kitchen knives, basic hand tools) are allowed but must be listed in compliance with category guidelines and local restrictions.

### 3.5 Adult and Explicit Content

- Pornographic material that violates local law or is not appropriate for a general audience marketplace.
- Sexually explicit products intended primarily for sexual stimulation.
- Obscene or excessively violent imagery.
- Products depicting or promoting sexual exploitation or abuse.

### 3.6 Hate, Violence, and Illegal Organizations

- Items that promote or glorify hatred, discrimination, or violence based on protected characteristics (e.g., race, religion, gender, nationality).
- Merchandise explicitly supporting organizations recognized as extremist or terrorist under applicable laws.
- Items with slogans or imagery that incite violence or harassment.

### 3.7 Human Remains and Biological Material

- Human organs, bones, or any part of the human body.
- Non-compliant animal parts, including items from endangered species.
- Biological samples requiring special handling or approvals not obtained by the seller.

### 3.8 Other Prohibited Categories

- Lottery tickets, gambling services, or betting systems where prohibited.
- Multi-level marketing schemes or investment opportunities presented as products.
- Products that are clearly deceptive or fraudulent (e.g., “magic” weight loss pills with impossible claims).

---

## 4. Restricted Products Requiring Approval

Certain product types may be listed only under additional conditions, such as prior approval, proof of authorization, or compliance documentation. Sellers must not list these items until approval is confirmed in the Seller Console.

### 4.1 Branded and Licensed Products

- High-risk brands prone to counterfeiting.
- Licensed merchandise (e.g., characters, sports teams) requiring proof of authorization.
- Luxury goods above internal risk thresholds.

Required documentation may include:

- Invoices from authorized distributors.
- Licensing agreements.
- Certificates of authenticity.

### 4.2 Health, Beauty, and Personal Care Products

- Over-the-counter medicines and medical devices where legally permitted.
- Cosmetics and skincare products with regulated ingredients.
- Nutritional supplements.

Sellers may need to provide:

- Ingredient lists and safety data sheets.
- Regulatory approvals or notifications where required.
- Proper labeling in the language of the destination country.

### 4.3 Electrical and Electronic Products

- Mains-powered devices and chargers.
- High-power tools and appliances.
- Smart devices with wireless connectivity.

Requirements typically include:

- Evidence of conformity with relevant electrical safety standards.
- Proper labeling (e.g., voltage, certifications, warnings).
- User manuals in the language(s) required in destination markets.

### 4.4 Home, Garden, and DIY Risk Categories

Certain items in the Home / Garden / DIY categories may be restricted, including:

- Pesticides, herbicides, and rodent control products.
- High-powered tools (e.g., chainsaws, table saws) requiring safety certifications.
- Pressure vessels (e.g., gas cylinders, certain garden sprayers).
- Products designed for installation on gas, water, or electrical systems requiring professional installation.

Category-specific requirements are detailed in the Category Guidelines (Home / Garden / DIY).

### 4.5 Food and Beverages

If Marketplace X enables food and beverage sales in a specific region:

- Only pre-packaged products with clear labeling may be allowed.
- Sellers must comply with expiry date, storage, and allergen labelling regulations.
- Some perishable items may be restricted due to logistics constraints.

---

## 5. Product Claims, Labelling, and Marketing Restrictions

### 5.1 Misleading or Unsubstantiated Claims

Sellers must not make claims that are:

- False, exaggerated, or impossible to verify.
- Health or medical claims not supported by regulatory approvals.
- Environmental or sustainability claims without clear basis (e.g., “100% eco-friendly” without evidence).

All claims must be truthful, evidence-based, and compliant with advertising laws.

### 5.2 Safety Warnings and Age Restrictions

- Products intended for children must include appropriate age recommendations and warnings (e.g., choking hazards).
- Products with inherent risks (e.g., power tools, sharp objects, chemicals) must include safety instructions and required labels.
- Age-restricted items (where allowed) must include clear age requirements and may require age verification measures.

### 5.3 Packaging and Documentation

- Required regulatory labels and marks must appear on both the product and packaging where applicable.
- Instructions for use, safety information, and warranty terms must be provided in the appropriate language(s).
- Repackaging must not remove or obscure regulatory labels or safety warnings.

---

## 6. Geographic and Cross-Border Restrictions

### 6.1 Regional Availability

Marketplace X may restrict certain products to specific countries or regions due to:

- Legal differences.
- Logistics and transport restrictions.
- Local marketplace rules.

Sellers must ensure that each listing’s shipping settings are aligned with the allowed destinations.

### 6.2 Customs and Import Restrictions

For cross-border shipments, sellers are responsible for:

- Understanding customs rules and prohibited items for destination countries.
- Accurate and complete customs declarations.
- Ensuring that items are not seized or destroyed due to non-compliance.

Marketplace X may disable cross-border shipping for specific product types if risk is deemed too high.

---

## 7. Monitoring, Reporting, and Enforcement

### 7.1 Marketplace X Monitoring

Marketplace X may use automated and manual processes to:

- Scan listings for prohibited or restricted content.
- Review listings flagged by customers, rights owners, or regulators.
- Request documentation from sellers to verify compliance.

Products may be suspended or removed while investigations are ongoing.

### 7.2 Reporting Violations

- Customers, rights owners, and other stakeholders can report suspected policy violations.
- Sellers who identify prohibited or potentially dangerous products on the marketplace are encouraged to report them to Marketplace X.

### 7.3 Enforcement Actions

Violations of this policy may result in:

- Immediate removal of non-compliant listings.
- Cancellation of open orders.
- Withholding of funds related to suspected violations.
- Additional verification requirements.
- Account-level penalties, including suspension or termination, as described in the Penalty System & Enforcement policy.

---

## 8. Responsibility and Updates

Sellers remain fully responsible for ensuring their products comply with all applicable laws and this policy, even if Marketplace X initially allows a listing to go live.

Marketplace X may update this Prohibited & Restricted Products policy at any time. Sellers must regularly review updates and adjust their catalog accordingly. Where in doubt, sellers should seek professional legal advice before listing sensitive or regulated items.
